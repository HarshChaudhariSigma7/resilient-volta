About

A notebook, not a database

A growing collection of articles on propulsion, aerodynamics, engineering design, and the ideas behind them. Each piece is written to explain the reasoning, not just the conclusion.

Alongside the writing are browser-based engineering tools in development, from simulation utilities to design assistants that expose assumptions, confidence, and trade-offs instead of treating engineering as a black box.

"The physics was never the mystery. The assumptions were."

The Method

Every case study reads the historical and current record through a strict four-part structured frame:

01 Known The documented physics, telemetry, and established equations.
02 Assumed The judgments, tolerances, and tuning that lived in one person's head.
03 Missing The unwritten records, lost parameters, and undocumented steps.
04 Implication The consequence where standard models diverge from physical reality.
